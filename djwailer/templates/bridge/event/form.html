Submit an Event

Complete the form below to submit your event to the Carthage Calendar.

WHERE WILL MY EVENT SHOW UP?

Your event will appear in the main Carthage calendar at www.carthage.edu/calendar, and in the Event list on The Bridge website. It may be selected for publication on additional webpages, such as the Carthage Homepage or academic department pages. Events are NOT included in the twice-weekly Bridge email. If you would like your event included in the email, please submit a news story too.

WHEN WILL MY EVENT BE PUBLISHED?

If you are a Carthage faculty or staff member, it will show up immediately. If you are a Carthage student, your event will show up once it has been approved by a moderator in the Office of Student Life.

WHEN SHOULD I SUBMIT MY EVENT?

You can submit your event as soon as you have all the details—the name of your event, description, time, date, place, and cost.

I'D LIKE TO INCLUDE A PHOTO OR POSTER.

The Bridge submission form does not currently accept photos, but if you have a photo you would like included in your listing, you may email it as an attachment to bridge@carthage.edu. Please put "Photo for Calendar Listing" in your subject line, and make sure you include the name and date of the event.
