Submit a News Item

Complete the form below to submit a news story to The Bridge.

WHERE WILL MY STORY SHOW UP?

Your story will appear in on The Bridge within the section you select: News & Notices, Arts & Performances, Kudos, Lectures & Presentations, Student News, etc. It may be selected for publication on additional webpages, such as the Carthage Homepage or academic department pages. Your news story will NOT appear in the main Carthage calendar. If you are publicizing an event, and you'd like it to appear in the calendar, please submit an event too.

WHEN WILL MY STORY BE PUBLISHED?

If you are a Carthage faculty or staff member, it will show up immediately. If you are a Carthage student, your news story will show up once it has been approved by a moderator in the Office of Student Life.

WHEN SHOULD I SUBMIT MY NEWS STORY?

Timing is completely up to you, but please keep in mind that your news story will be included in the next Bridge email roundup. So if you are publicizing an event, it may be best to wait until the date of your event is 2-4 days away. If you'd rather announce an exciting event now ("Carthage is bringing a new lecture series to campus!"), go ahead. But then remember to share the news again as the event nears ("New lecture series presents first lecture on Friday").

I'D LIKE TO INCLUDE A PHOTO OR POSTER.

The Bridge submission form does not currently accept photos, but if you have a photo you would like included in your news story, you may email it as an attachment to bridge@carthage.edu. Please put "Photo for News Story" in your subject line, and make sure you include the headline of the story.
